FULLY FUNDED Scholarship

Scholarships provide essential financial support, allowing students to pursue their education without the burden of debt. They open doors to higher education that might otherwise be inaccessible, enabling students to focus on their studies and excel academically. Additionally, scholarships bring prestige and recognition, enhancing resumes and career prospects. Lastly, they offer networking opportunities that can lead to valuable connections and future career success.

Private Sectors

JN Tata Endowment for the Higher Education of Indians

The JN Tata Endowment for the Higher Education of Indians is a prestigious loan scholarship offered by Tata Trusts to Indian students pursuing postgraduate, doctoral, or postdoctoral studies abroad. Applicants must be Indian nationals, graduates from recognized Indian universities, and should demonstrate academic excellence and potential for future contributions to society -

Reliance Foundation Scholarships

The Reliance Foundation Scholarship supports Indian students pursuing undergraduate and postgraduate studies in fields like technology, innovation, and management, offering financial assistance, mentorship, and career development opportunities -

Tata Capital Pankh Scholarship Program

The Tata Capital Pankh Scholarship Program provides financial assistance to meritorious students from economically weaker sections pursuing school (Class 6-12), diploma, and undergraduate courses in India. Applicants must demonstrate academic excellence with a minimum required percentage and belong to a family with an annual income below a specified threshold. - For Register

HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECSS Programme

The HDFC Bank Parivartan’s Educational Crisis Scholarship Support (ECSS) Programme provides financial assistance to students facing personal or family crises, ensuring continuity in their education. Eligible students must be enrolled in school (Class 6-12) or undergraduate/postgraduate courses in India, with preference given to those from economically weaker sections. -

For Register

Bharti Airtel Scholarship Program

The Bharti Airtel Scholarship Program supports academically talented students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds pursuing higher education in India. Applicants must demonstrate strong academic performance and come from families with a low annual income, with a focus on those pursuing undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in various fields. -

For Register

Government Sectors

Post Matric Scholarship for Students with Disabilities

The Post Matric Scholarship for Students with Disabilities provides financial assistance to students with disabilities pursuing post-secondary education in India. Eligibility requires the applicant to have a disability certificate, be enrolled in a recognized post-matriculation course, and belong to economically weaker sections of society. -

For Register

AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girls 2024-25

The AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girls 2024-25 supports female students pursuing technical education at the diploma or undergraduate level in India. Eligibility includes being a first-year student with a family income below ₹8 lakhs per annum and having secured admission to an AICTE-approved institution. -

For Register

Swami Vivekananda Merit Cum Means Scholarship for Minorities, West Bengal

The Swami Vivekananda Merit Cum Means Scholarship for Minorities, West Bengal provides financial aid to minority students pursuing higher education in West Bengal. Eligibility requires students to demonstrate academic excellence, have a family income below ₹2.5 lakhs per annum, and be enrolled in a recognized course at an undergraduate or postgraduate level. - For Register

Post-matric Scholarship for Disabled, Maharashtra

The Post-matric Scholarship for Disabled, Maharashtra offers financial support to students with disabilities pursuing post-secondary education in Maharashtra. Eligibility requires the applicant to have a valid disability certificate, be enrolled in a recognized post-matriculation course, and belong to an economically weaker section with a family income below a specified threshold. -

For Register

NEC Merit Scholarship NER

The NEC Merit Scholarship NER supports students from the North-Eastern Region of India pursuing higher education. Eligibility includes being a resident of one of the North-Eastern states, having secured a minimum percentage in the previous qualifying exam, and enrolling in a recognized undergraduate or postgraduate course. -

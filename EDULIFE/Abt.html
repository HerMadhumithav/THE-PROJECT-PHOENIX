....About Us....


  • In Today’s situation, students have the potential to study, perform various other activities still there are some obstacles which prevent them from reaching their goal. We're here to implement a solution to their problem through our idea called “THE PROJECT PHOENIX”
  • This project depicts the idea of gathering youth minds and guiding the students who struggle to balance their educational career as well as personal life.
  • We would like to proceed forward this idea through our app called “EDULIFE”
  • This app mainly focuses on the well-being of a student who’s struggling to manage their education career along with personal life added constraints(medical stability, financial crisis, time management, accessibility to resources etc..,)

  • Organization is tied up with necessary trusts(educational institution, medical institution, fund raisers).By this, we’ll be able to help the student by
  • * Setting remainders-Time managemen
  • * Educational resources(available online sources)
  • * Yoga and exercises that helps to maintain the mental stability
  • * Also, fun activities remainders, additional sport facilities are also provided

EduLife is an online learning platform that offers a wide range of courses, resources, and tools to support students, teachers, and institutions in achieving their educational goals
